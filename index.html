About us

About Marites Express

Marites Express is a courier service dream created by five friends. Erika, Reizelle, Judy, Julie, and Justin — who met while taking the Certificate in Office Administration. Their friendship grew through a shared vision: to build a trusted and reliable delivery service in Metro Manila. Although Marites Express is still in the planning stage, our goal is to one day operate with a strong team of 40 riders who will provide fast, secure, and dependable deliveries. This is just the beginning, and we’re excited for what’s ahead.
